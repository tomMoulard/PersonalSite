3. Dialogue

(1) How to Rent a House?

Nǐ zhù nǎr ?

David: 你住哪儿？

Where do you live?

Wǒ xiànzài zhù jiāotāngdàxué, zhèng xiǎng zū fáng zi , nǐ zhī dào zěn me zū ma ?

Anna: 我现在住交通大学，正想租房子，你知道怎么租吗？

I am staying in the Jiaotong University. I want to rent a house. Can you help me?

Nǐ kěyǐ zhǎo zhōngjiè gōngsī .

David: 你可以找中介公司。

You should find an estate agency.

Nǐ yǒu zhōngjiè gōng sī de diànhuà ma ?

Anna: 你有中介公司的电话吗？

Do you have any agencies telephone number?

Méiyǒu. Wǒ bāng nǐ wènwen ba .

David: 没有。我帮你问问吧。

No. I will check for you.


(2) How About This Flat?

Wǒ xiǎng zū yí tào sān shì yī tīng de fáng zi .

Anna: 我想租一套三室一厅的房子。

I would like to rent an apartment with three bedrooms and one living room.

Zhè tào zěnmeyàng?

Agent: 这套怎么样？

How about this one?

Hái xíng , zhè tào zū jīn shì duō shǎo ?

Anna: 还行，这套租金是多少？

Not bad, How much is the rent for this one?

Yí gè yuè sānqiān yuán.

Agent: 一个月三千元。

3000 yuan per month.


(3) The Room Is Very Hot!

Nǐde fángjiān lǐ zhēn rè !

David : 你的房间里真 热！

It is very hot in your room.

Fángjiān lǐ de kōngtiáo huàile.

Anna: 房间里的空调坏了。

The air conditioner is not working.

Nǐ gěi fángdōng dǎ ge diànhuà ba, tā huì zhǎo rén lái xiū.

David: 你给房东打个电话吧，他会找人来修 .

You should give the house owner a call. He will find someone to fix it.

Hǎo ， wǒ xiànzài jiù dǎ .

Anna : 好，我现在就打。

Sure, I will make the call now.


(4) By Taxi

Zhège zhōumò wǒ gēn jiékè yìqǐ qù gùgōng ,nǐ xiǎng qù ma?

David : 这个周末我跟杰克一起去故宫，你想去吗？

I am going to the Forbidden City with Jack the weekend. Do you want to join us?

Hǎo a, wǒ yě xiǎng gēn nǐmen yìqǐ qù. zěnme qù ne ？

Anna ：好啊，我也想跟你们一起去。怎么去 呢？

Yes, I would like to go with you both. How can we get there?

Wǒ men xiǎng dǎdī qù ？

David: 我们想打的去。

I plan to go by taxi.

Zuò dìtiě ba, yòu kuài yòu piányi.

Anna ：坐地铁吧，又快又便宜。

How about going by subway? It is fast and cheap.


(5) How to get to the subway station?

Nínhǎo ！ Xiǎojiě ， qǐngwèn dào dìtiězhàn zěnme zǒu ？

Anna: 您好！小姐，请问到地铁站怎么走？

Hello! Miss, could you tell me how to get to the subway station.

Cóng zhèr yízhí wǎngqián zǒu, dào dì èr ge lùkǒu wǎng yòu guǎi jiù dào le .

Lady: 从这儿一直往前走，到第二个路口往右拐就到了。

Go straight ahead and turn right at the second junction.

Xièxiè ！

Anna: 谢谢！

Thank you!

Búkèqi ！

Lady : 不客气！

You are welcome!


（ 6 ） Where Is the Supermarket ？

Jiékè ， nǐ zhīdao nǎr yǒu chāoshì ma ？

David : 杰克，你知道哪儿有超市吗？

Jack, do you know where the supermarket is?

Qián miàn jiù yǒu ge dà chāo shì,hái yǒu jǐ gè xiǎo shāngdiàn ne.

Jack ：前面就有个大超市，还有几个小商店呢。

There is a large supermarket up ahead, and there are several convenience stores as well.

lí zhèr yuǎn ma ？

David : 离这儿远吗？

Is it far from here?

Hěnjìn, dàgài liǎngbǎi mǐ.

Jack ：很近，大概两百米。

Very close, about two hundred meters.

Xièxie ！

David ：谢谢！

Thank you


（ 7 ） How Much Are These Apples ？

Píngguǒ zěnme mài ？

David : 苹果怎么卖？

How much are these apples?

Wǔ kuài wǔ yìjīn.

Seller: 五块五一斤。

5.5Yuan for half a kilo.

Tàiguì le, piányi diǎnr ba.

David : 太贵了，便宜点儿吧。

Too expensive. How about a little discount.

Wǔkuài ba. Nǐ mǎi duō shǎo ？

Seller: 五块吧。你买多少？

5 Yuan. How many do you need?


（ 8 ） Would You Like Anything else?

Háiyào bié de ma ？

Seller: 还要别的吗？

Anything else?

Búyào le. Yígòng duōshǎo qián ？

David : 不要了。一共多少钱？

Not more. How much is it in total.

Shísān kuài wǔ, nǐ gěi shísān kuài ba.

Seller: 十三块五，你给十三块吧。

13.5Yuan, you can just give me 13.

Gěinǐ yìbǎi kuài, wǒ méiyǒu língqián.

David : 给你 100 块，我没有零钱。

Here is a hundred Yuan. I don’t have small change.

Zhǎonǐ bāshí qī kuài, nǐ shǔshu.

Seller: 找你 87 块，你数数。

I will give you 87Yuan in change, please count it.


（ 9 ） Please Come In

Qǐngjìn qǐng wèn nǐmen jǐ wèi ？

fú wù yuán : 请进，请问你们几位？

Come in please. How many people?

Sānwèi . Yǒu bāojiān ma ？

kè rén: 三位。有包间吗？

Three. Do you have any private rooms?

Duìbùqǐ ， méiyǒu le. Zuò dàtīng kěyǐ ma ？

fú wù yuán : 对不起，没有了。坐大厅可以吗？

I am sorry, we don’t. Could you sit in the hall?

Méi wèntí.

kè rén: 没问题。

No problem.


(10) Order Dishes

Fúwùyuán, diǎn cài .

kè rén: 服务员，点菜。

Waiter, can we order now?

Hǎo de . Zhèshì càidān. Qǐng wèn nǐmen chī diǎnr shénme ？

fú wù yuán : 好的。这是菜单。请问你们吃点儿什么？
